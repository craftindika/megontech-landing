Check-In & Check-Out

90 Seconds. That's All It Takes.

From the moment your guest walks in to the moment they have their key, Horizon handles every step in under 90 seconds.

1

Guest Arrives

Pull up the reservation in one tap. Name, room type, special requests, all visible instantly. No searching, no asking the guest to repeat themselves.

2

ID Verified

Scan or upload the ID. Nationality, document number, and photo, captured in a single screen. Fully compliant with local regulations.

3

Room Assigned

Horizon suggests the best available room based on guest preferences, floor, view, and housekeeping status. One click to confirm.

4

Key Issued

Key card encoded or digital key sent. Housekeeping is auto-notified. The guest is on their way, smiling.

5

Folio & Check-Out

Auto-generated folio with every charge. Guest reviews, settles (cash, card, UPI), and receives a digital receipt. Under 60 seconds.
